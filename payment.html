📚How to Buy Crypto — Step by Step Guide
1
Create a Binance Account
Go to binance.com and sign up with your email. Complete identity verification (KYC) by uploading your MyKad or Passport — this takes 5–10 minutes.
2
Deposit Malaysian Ringgit (MYR)
Go to Buy Crypto → P2P Trading. Select MYR as your fiat currency. Choose a verified seller and transfer via DuitNow/online banking. You'll receive USDT in your Binance wallet.
3
Buy Your Preferred Crypto
In Binance Spot market, you can swap your USDT to BTC, ETH, or USDC instantly at market rate. Go to Trade → Spot → search your coin.
4
Withdraw to Nexora AI Wallet
Go to Wallet → Withdraw. Select your coin, paste the Nexora AI wallet address, enter the exact amount, select the correct network (ERC-20 for ETH/USDC, TRC-20 for USDT, BTC for Bitcoin), and confirm.
Always select the correct network when withdrawing! ERC-20 for ETH/USDC, TRC-20 for USDT, BTC network for Bitcoin. Wrong network = lost funds.
