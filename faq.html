Technical

We guarantee 99.7% uptime for all AI robots. Our infrastructure is built on enterprise-grade servers with automated failover and redundancy. In the rare event of downtime, Enterprise clients receive compensation per our SLA terms.

Strategy customisation is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. You can adjust risk parameters, operating hours, and specific channels from your dashboard. Our team assists Enterprise clients in building fully custom strategies.

All plans include a real-time dashboard where you can monitor your AI's activity, earnings, and performance metrics. Professional plans receive weekly automated reports, and Enterprise clients get daily reports plus a dedicated account manager.

Payment

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), online banking transfer (FPX), TNG eWallet, and direct bank transfer. Enterprise clients can also pay via invoice with net-30 payment terms.

For monthly plans, you're billed on the same date each month from when you subscribed. For annual plans, you're billed once per year. You'll receive a receipt by email after each successful payment.

Professional plan subscribers can request a full refund within 14 days of their first payment. For subsequent billing cycles, refunds are not available. Enterprise clients receive a 30-day guarantee. Please contact support to process a refund request.
